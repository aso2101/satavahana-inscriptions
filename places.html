Sātavāhana Gazetteer

This is a list of all of the places either mentioned in the corpus of inscriptions, or where the inscriptions have actually been found. In some cases a place falls into both categories. The places are listed by their ancient names. (Note that some places referred to in these texts, such as the names of modern villages or states, are skipped here.)

Some places, but not all, have a short description which can be accessed by clicking on the link. Where available, places are put on the below map.
